Profile

Robotics-minded mechanical engineering, from prototype to tested system.

My interest in mechanisms began with LEGO EV3 projects and grew through science fairs, FRC robotics, and university-level engineering projects. At National Taiwan University, I have led teams in quadruped robot, quadcopter, and ball-transfer mechanism projects. These projects trained me to connect design intent with manufacturing constraints, electrical layout, control logic, and test results.

I enjoy the part of engineering where uncertainty becomes a list of concrete tests. When a mechanism shakes, a motor behaves unexpectedly, or a team is running out of time, I try to break the problem into measurable causes, assign work clearly, and keep the system moving toward a reliable final result.
